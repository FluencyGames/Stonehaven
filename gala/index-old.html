209 Stonehaven Gala

The Stonehaven School's 2019 Gala will be held on Friday, October 25 at The Brickyard in Marietta.

What happens at the Gala?

The Gala is one of our school's annual fundraising events which will include dinner, entertainment, and a silent auction. It is a wonderful opportunity to celebrate our school and strengthen our community. This is an adults only event, so book your babysitter today.

Can I invite friends outside of the Stonehaven community?

Yes! This is great event to invite prospective families to or anyone who may have an interest in learning more about our school. You do have the option of sponsoring a table if you'd like to host a few friends. If you have questions about table sponsorships, please contact Melissa Dickinson (mdickinson@stonehavenschool.org).

What is the dress code?

Cocktail attire is recommended.

What time does the Gala start?

The Gala will begin at 6:30pm.

How do I purchase tickets?

We are currently sold out for this year's Gala!

Where should I park for the Gala?

Free parking is available in the small lot immediately adjacent to the Brickyard and on the surrounding streets. Paid parking is available across the street at First Baptist Church or in the parking decks around the Marietta Square. Please allow a few minutes to find parking.

If you have any additional questions about the Gala, please contact Melissa Dickinson - mdickinson@stonehavenschool.org.
