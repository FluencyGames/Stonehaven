Board Commitments

– biblically-based integrity and truthfulness
– externally focused vision and planning, rather than an internally focused preoccupation
– strategic leadership more than administrative detail preoccupation
– clear distinction of Board and Head of School roles
– collective, rather than individual, decision-making
– future rather than past or present policy-making orientation
– proactively rather than reactivity
