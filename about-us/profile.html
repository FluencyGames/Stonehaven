School Profile

Home >About Us >Profile

Established in 2004, the school's original name was Atlanta Classical Christian Academy. In the spring of 2015, the school changed its name to The Stonehaven School. The name reflects the school's foundation upon the rock of Jesus Christ (Stone) and a culture of grace, love, and rest (Haven).

  • Established: 2004
  • Grades: Kindergarten - 9th Grade*
  • School Type: Private, Co-educational, Independent Christian Day School, Board-governed
  • Enrollment: 160 Students
  • Average Class Size: 18
  • Accreditation: Southern Association of Independent Schools (SAIS)
  • Membership: Association of Classical Christian Schools
  • Hours: Kindergarten (Part Time): 8:15am - 12:00pm
    Kindergarten - 6th Grade: 8:15am - 3:00pm
    7th Grade - 9th Grade: 8:30am - 3:15pm
    After School Program: 3:00am - 6:00pm
  • No Distinction: The Stonehaven School makes no distinction concerning an individual's race, color, or ethnic background because the school acknowledges that all persons are created in God's image (Genesis 1:27), and He makes no such distinctions (Galatians 3:28) in his redemptive plan for mankind.

    • * Stonehaven launched a ninth grade program in August of 2020 and will continue adding one grade per year through the twelfth grade by August of 2023.
