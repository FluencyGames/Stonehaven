Continuous Enrollment

Home > Admissions > Continuous Enrollment

Wanting to simplify how families re-enroll their children, Stonehaven uses a process called Continuous Enrollment. In fact, there is no need at all to re-enroll. Once your children are enrolled, they are continuously enrolled until graduation from Stonehaven or the family follows an opt-out procedure.

To state the process as simply as possible:

  • As part of the first-time enrollment process for new students, parents will indicate their understanding that their children are continuously enrolled at Stonehaven until graduation or, if necessary, the opt-out process is followed.
  • In March, a $500 Continuous Enrollment fee per family will be automatically charged via FACTS and applied towards next year's tuition.
  • Once enrolled, students are considered part of Stonehaven until they graduate with no further annual "re-enrollment" process involved.

We have created a list of Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) to help our families understand how this process works.

The introduction of this process has been well-received at Stonehaven. The message we want to send is this: we want your children to receive a Christ-centered and classical education for many years to come and will make their continued enrollment as easy and seamless as possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Continuous Enrollment?

  • Continuous Enrollment is a common-sense approach to enrollment which is followed by almost all colleges and is being adopted by many Christian schools nation-wide.
  • Rather than an opt-IN annual re-enrollment process, Continuous Enrollment is an opt-OUT process. In other words, if you're returning to Stonehaven after initially enrolling, you’ll never have to worry about re-enrolling.

What is the Continuous Enrollment annual timeline?

  • January - tuition is published for the upcoming year. Opt-out period begins
  • February - Indexed Tuition application period begins. Opt-out period ends February 28
  • March - Continuous Enrollment fee is withdrawn via FACTS for all enrolled students, unless an opt-out form is received

Does our family need a FACTS account?

  • Yes, all families will need to have a FACTS account for monthly tuition collection. In addition to automatically collecting tuition payments, FACTS manages the collection of the Continuous Enrollment fee and other fees as needed.
  • Those families who already have a FACTS account do not need to register for one again. Those who do not should do so in January or February. The process is online and simple.
  • Families who are on a one- or three-payment plan may submit their tuition payments directly to the Stonehaven business office without using FACTS. However, the Continuous Enrollment fee will be collected through FACTS unless specific arrangements are made with the business office.

Is the Continuous Enrollment fee refundable?

Yes, but only in the following cases:

  • If your family submits an opt-out form by Feb 28 (in which case the fee will not be drawn)
  • In the event of a move out of the area before school begins
  • In the event that Stonehaven can no longer meet your child’s educational needs
  • In the event that Stonehaven asks your child to withdraw
  • In the event that your Indexed Tuition amount is insufficient, after Stonehaven reviews your FACTS application with you to consider extenuating circumstances
  • In the event that there are unforeseen circumstances, in which case a refund needs Board approval

How do I opt out of Continuous Enrollment?

  • January and February are the opt-out months for current Stonehaven families. During that time, families who need to opt-out of Continuous Enrollment may do so by filling out an online opt-out form, found here.

What if I opt out and later decide to re-enroll?

  • Families who opt-out and later decide to re-enroll will be considered for enrollment along with new applicants and according to space available.
