School Tours & Open Houses

Home > Admissions > School Tours & Open Houses

The best way to learn about the distinctive features of Stonehaven is to visit us. See the schedule below to register for an Open House or arrange a time for an individual tour. On your visit you will get to tour the campus, observe classrooms, and see Stonehaven's classical and Christian education in action. Tours help prospective parents gain a deeper understanding of the unique elements of classical Christian education and the joyful culture at Stonehaven. Visitors will also have the opportunity to meet our remarkable teachers and ask any questions they might have about our program. We look forward to your visit.

Frequently Asked Questions

What will I see at an Open House?

Open Houses begin with an introduction from the Head of School and a short talk about the school's Christ-centered classical mission and vision. We then break up into groups allowing each group to take a more customized school tour. Tours will drop in on relevant classes to observe presentations that provide parents with an idea of what students are learning in the classroom. During the tours, parents are encouraged to ask questions of their guides to learn more about Stonehaven.

What will I see on a private tour?

We can cater school tours to the interests and desires of each prospective family. Generally, tours will visit the classrooms related to the child's "rising" grade level. Tour of the music room, art room, cafeteria, playground, and sanctuary. We also like to sit down with prospective families at the end and address any and all questions from the parents.

How do I register for an open house?

Prospective families are encouraged (but not required) to RSVP for the Open House.

How do I arrange a school tour?

The easiest way to arrange a school tour is to call Admissions Director Hannah Miley at (770) 874-8885 (ext 202). Tours can also be arranged by emailing admissions@stonehavenschool.org.

How long is an open house?

Prospective parents usually spend a minimum of 60 minutes and up to 90 minutes at Open Houses.

How long is a school tour?

School tours generally take between 45 and 60 minutes.

Parking & Front Office

Stonehaven is located at GracePointe Marietta Baptist Church at 505 Atlanta Street. Visitors are encouraged to park in the back parking lot. The main white doors are the entrance for both the church office and the school office. Press the school call button to reach our front office for entry into the building. Walk upstairs to the second floor where you will find the front office to the school.

Before Your Visit

We encourage interested parents to explore our website to read more about what makes Stonehaven unique and different. This often helps prepare families with the right questions for their visit to Stonehaven.

After Your Visit

Are you still interested after taking a tour of Stonehaven. We invite you to complete an application to Stonehaven and arrange a shadow day for your child.
