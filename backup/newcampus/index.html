New Campus

Joyner Avenue Update

Our Board of Trustees is excited to announce that we have signed a contract (a purchase-sale agreement) to buy a church property located at 1480 Joyner Avenue in Marietta. This was an answer to our fervent prayers to find a future home for our Kindergarten through sixth grade students. The property is two acres and the building has 23,000 square feet of space. The building will provide 15+ classrooms, a large sanctuary, cafeteria, and playground space.

We started a due diligence process in September that will continue through December. The objectives during the due diligence process are to complete various inspections and surveys to ensure the property and building are suitable for our school. We also will submit a SLUP (special land use permit) application to Cobb County that will seek approval to use the property for the purposes of a school. This application will be considered by the county in December.

Our plan is to renovate the space from January through May, move into the building in June and July, and beging the 2021-22 school year (K-6th) in the new building. Soon we will be launching a capital campaign to help raise the necessary funds for the renovation project.

We look forward to providing updates in the coming months regarding the vision and plan for our new school home. Grateful...
