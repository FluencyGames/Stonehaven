Giving To Stonehaven

Home > Giving to Stonehaven

The value of a Stonehaven education reaches far beyond the classroom; that distinction lies in our deep tradition of community. Small, nurturing and dynamic, our family or parents, alumni, grandparents, faculty, staff, and friends increases the value of the Stonehaven experience each year through its spirit of volunteerism and giving.

Like most independent schools, tuition and fees do not cover the total cost of educating your student, and so additional financial support from all members of the Stonehaven community is key. And, as is the case with other independent schools, Stonehaven solicits tax-deductible gifts and special financial support from its constituencies in several ways.

We are here to help!

If you have questions about the type of contribution you are interested in making to Stonehaven, give our Development Coordinator, Melissa Dickinson, a call at 770-874-8885. She is happy to speak with you about the many ways you can make a difference in the lives of Stonehaven students.
