Enrichment Opportunities

Stonehaven offers various enrichment activities each year that provide students an opportunity to develop particular talents beyond the classroom. The programs are designed to serve the school's mission by cultivating Christian virtues like diligence, creativity, discipline, teamwork, fortitude, and joy. Click on the links below to learn more about the details of the enrichment opportunities for the 2021-2022 school year.

Drama Club

Sponsor: Stonehaven Drama Club
Age Level: 3rd-10th grade
Cost: $50/month
Season: September 7 - April
Days: Tuesdays
Time: 3:30-5:30
Location: Upper School Campus
Register: Submit a Drama Club Form to express interest. Registration will be available soon.
Description: The Drama Club will be working on a production of The Sound of Music!

Piano

Teacher: Kelsey Blackman
Age Level: K-8th Grade beginner level
Cost: $25/session plus the cost of books
Season: February-April
Days: Thursdays (and Tuesdays if interest)
Time: 30 minute sessions beginning at 3:15
Register: contact Mrs. Blackman at 501-605-7335 or email at kelseyvaughn12@gmail.com
Note: ukulele or French Horn lessons are also available. Please contact if interested.

Study Lab

Tutor: Upper School Teachers
Age Level: 7th-10th
Cost: $7 per session or $20 per month
Contact: Mr. Proffitt
Description: The after school study lab creates a quiet space for students to work on their academics, receive tutoring help from teachers, and develop healthy study habits. Currently, we are offering the study lab in the Upper School for students in 7th through 10th grade.
