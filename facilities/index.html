Stonehaven Facility Use

God has been abundantly gracious in his provision for our school. We now have two properties and buildings that we can use to love and serve our neighbors. This page is intended to help provide information and coordination for the use of Stonehaven's facilities.

The book of Hebrews says, "Do not neglect to do good and to share what you have, for such sacrifices are pleasing to God" (Hebrews 13:16).

We are pleased that you are interested in using Stonehaven facilities. We are committed to using our facilities to the glory of God. However, it is important that we are good stewards of what He has entrusted to us. Therefore, we are not able to approve all requests to use Stonehaven facilities.

For those interested in using the sanctuary at Joyner Avenue, wedding services are available to all who desire a sacred union with an emphasis on obtaining a Christian marriage.

General Guidelines

  • Groups using Stonehaven's facilities must do so in alignment with the school's Christian statement of faith.
  • A Stonehaven employee or representative must be present during the event.
  • Groups seeking to use the facility for profit will be required to pay a portion of the profit to the school.
  • All groups will be required to pay a nominal fee for cleaning.

Facility Use Application Process

  1. Step One - Confirm the desired date(s) and times are available on the facility use calendar below. It is possible that events can overlap on the same day/time depending upon the specific facility and location within the building.
  2. Complete Facility Request Form - Complete the Facility Request Form which will be emailed to Stonehaven's administration.
  3. Stonehaven Responds to Request - Stonehaven will either accept or deny the request by email or phone within seven days.
  4. Calendar Confirmation - It is important to note that the request has not been fully confirmed until it appears on the Google calendar below. Those requesting use must verify the request by checking the calendar. This is to ensure that Stonehaven has not made any mistakes regarding the date, time, and location for the request.
