The Time Is Now Capital Campaign

In August of 2020, the Lord opened another providential door giving our school an opportunity to fully secure a permanent school home for our Lower School.

In late July, we learned that a 23,000 square foot church was for sale just 1/2 of a mile from our new Upper School campus. We were busy praying for and making a plan for how we could secure a permanent home for our Lower School program. We soon learned this would be an ideal location for Stonehaven's Lower School. We have been fervently praying that the Lord would make a way for our school to find a place for us to begin planting roots and investing in the community. The building will provide 15 classrooms, office space, a cafeteria, a large sanctuary, and additional space for future expansion.

The Time Is Now Campaign will provide the necessary funding to renovate the classrooms and cafeteria, fully redo bathrooms, repave the parking lots, and build a new playground. The new building at Joyner Avenue allows Stonehaven to fulfill its mission in four crucial areas:

  • Provide a stable, secure location for our Kindergarten through sixth grade
  • Ability to expand to two sections of each grade in our Lower School
  • Provides flexibility and freedom to dream and build programs that will enhance our Christ-centered classical program
  • Provides an accessible location close to our Upper School property
